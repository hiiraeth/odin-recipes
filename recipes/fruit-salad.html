Perfect Summer Fruit Salad

Description

Easy fruit salad that's bright, colorful, delicious, and perfect for those hot days.

Ingredients

For the Sauce

  • Fresh orange and lemon juices
  • Brown sugar
  • Grated orange and lemon zests
  • Vanilla Extract

Fruit needed

  • Pineapple
  • Strawberries
  • Kiwis
  • Bananas
  • Oranges
  • Grapes
  • Blueberries
  • ...or any fruit of your choosing!

Steps

  1. Make the sauce on the stove and let it cool.
  2. Arrange the fruits in a container, then pour the sauce over them
  3. Cover and refrigerate to allow the flavors to meld.
